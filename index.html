On Wednesday, October 30th, The Downtown School will be hosting the first Seattle Math Circle event.

This event is an opportunity for middle and high school math teachers at independent schools in the Seattle area to network and enjoy doing math with each other.

John Conway's Game of Life is a great example of a topic with a low entry and a high ceiling. By exploring the game, math teachers will practice their mathematical thinking and ability to ask good questions while having fun.

Stay tuned for more information about future Seattle Math Circles! Events will tentatively be once a month.
